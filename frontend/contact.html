Directions to The Coconut Grill Hotels

From Nairobi

  1. Take the Thika Superhighway (A2) heading north
  2. Exit at Thika town exit (approximately 45 minutes from Nairobi CBD)
  3. Follow signs to Thika town center
  4. Turn onto Kenyatta Highway - we're on the main road
  5. Look for The Coconut Grill Hotels signage

Approximately 45 minutes from Nairobi

By Public Transport

  1. Take a matatu or bus from Nairobi to Thika (Route 45, 237)
  2. Alight at Thika town center
  3. We're on Kenyatta Highway - a short walk or boda boda ride from the main stage
  4. Ask locals for "Coconut Grill" - we're well known!

Cost: Approximately KShs 100-150

From Mount Kenya Region

  1. Head south on the Nyeri-Thika road
  2. Continue into Thika town
  3. Turn onto Kenyatta Highway in the CBD
  4. The hotel is on the main highway

Approximately 1.5-2 hours from Nyeri

From JKIA Airport

  1. Exit the airport and head to Nairobi city
  2. Take the Eastern Bypass to Thika Road
  3. Join Thika Superhighway heading north
  4. Exit at Thika and follow to Kenyatta Highway

Approximately 1 hour 15 minutes

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I make a booking?

You can book by calling us at +254 700 000 000, WhatsApp, email, or filling out our contact form above. We respond quickly to all booking requests.

Do you offer airport transfers?

Yes! We can arrange airport pickup and drop-off with our trusted transport partners. Contact us in advance to arrange this service.

Is breakfast included?

Yes, complimentary breakfast is included with all room bookings. We serve both continental and Kenyan breakfast options daily from 7:00 AM to 10:00 AM.

Do you have free WiFi and parking?

Absolutely! Free high-speed WiFi and secure parking are complimentary for all our guests.

Can non-guests use the restaurant?

Yes! The Coconut Grill Restaurant is open to the public. Everyone is welcome to enjoy our delicious food and daily buffet lunch.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash (KES, USD, EUR, GBP), M-Pesa, Visa, Mastercard, and bank transfers for your convenience.

Can you host events and conferences?

Yes! We have conference halls for 50-300 delegates, complete with AV equipment. We also host weddings, parties, and other events. Contact us for packages.

Are children welcome?

Absolutely! We're family-friendly. Children under 13 stay free in our Family Suites, and we provide baby cots at no extra charge.
