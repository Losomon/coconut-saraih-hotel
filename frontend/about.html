Our Story

A Legacy of Coastal Hospitality

Nestled along the pristine shores of Coastal Kenya, The Coconut Saraih has been a beacon of warm hospitality and seaside luxury for over two decades.

What began as a family dream to share the beauty of our coastal homeland with the world has blossomed into one of Kenya's most beloved beachfront destinations. Our commitment to genuine care, authentic experiences, and sustainable tourism remains at the heart of everything we do.

Every corner of our property tells a story of tradition meeting contemporary comfort, where guests become family and moments become memories that last a lifetime.
